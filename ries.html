LLL-style algebraic relation finder

Given decimal precision, search for a low-degree, small-height integer polynomial approximately satisfied by the number.

Ready.

Method

This builds the standard integer-relation lattice for 1, x, x², …, xᵈ, applies a lightweight LLL reduction, and ranks relations by degree, coefficient height, and residual. Decimal inputs are scaled with BigInt arithmetic; expression inputs fall back to double precision.

x = 1.4142135623730950488
→ x² - 2 = 0

x = 1.32471795724474602596
→ x³ - x - 1 = 0
